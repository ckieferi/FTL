About us

The Fintech Ladies are a network for women in the finance and tech industry. Headquartered in Berlin, the network has expanded to various cities all over Europe. We currently operate in Germany, Austria, Switzerland and Belgium.

Digitisation and innovation are transforming the financial sector fundamentally. We believe that fintech is the future of finance. Our network is for women working in fintech startups, banks, law firms, financial services and consulting.
Together, we want to learn and grow, we want to inspire and empower each other.

We want to shape the future of finance.

Events

#FTLDeepdive

The Deep Dives are our regular networking events. We partner with companies who invite us to their office and host a session.
The intimate setting guarantees intensive networking opportunities.

#FTLAcademy

The Fintech Ladies Academy offers workshops on legal topics, coding, self-development and more. We are aiming to provide continuous education and knowledge sharing amongst fintech professionnels.

#FTLDinner

The annual Fintech Ladies Dinner is an exclusive dinner event where we gather women in senior positions, C-Level and founders.
Places are very limited.
Participation is by invite-only or per formal application.

#FintechStories

The Fintech Stories are open to everyone. We invite senior female executives from the fintech industry to talk about their company and their careerpath, followed by a round table discussion and a Q&A by the audience.
